Deviled Eggs

How to make deviled eggs

If you're looking for an introduction to deviled eggs, this is the place to start: just eggs, mustard, mayonnaise, a dash of Sriracha and a festive sprinkle of paprika (or jazz things up with a garnish of chives). They are a simple and spectacular addition to a holiday table.

Deviled eggs ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 to 2 dashes Sriracha sauce, to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp snipped fresh chives
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp relish
  • 1 tsp white vinegar or white wine vinegar
  • Paprika, for garnish
  • Whole fresh chives, for garnish

Once you gather all the ingredients to make the most angelic deviled eggs, here's how to do it:

How to hard boil eggs for devil eggs

  1. Carefully place eggs in an empty pot large enough to fit them all in one layer.
  2. Fill pot with cold tap water until eggs are covered.
  3. Place the pot over high heat and bring to a boil, allowing it to boil for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Turn the heat to low, cover the pot and let simmer for 17 minutes. Do not uncover the pot during this time.
  5. While eggs are simmer, prepare an ice bath. Once eggs are finished simmering, place directly into ice bath and let cool for 5 minutes.
